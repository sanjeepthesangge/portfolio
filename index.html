Passion for Learning

I am Sanjeep Lama — an educator, engineer, and community builder working at the crossroads of software craftsmanship and human potential. At Islington College, I teach Software Engineering and supervise final year Computing projects, guiding students from concept through completion.

My journey began in quality assurance automation at InnovateTech, where I developed a deep appreciation for systematic, disciplined engineering. That rigor now shapes everything I bring to the classroom — structured thinking, clean architecture, and the patience to teach why, not just how.

Outside academia, I mentor aspiring developers through the Inosphere WebDev Community, where the best learning happens in community — shared struggle, shared growth.
